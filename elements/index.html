Doodle Tagelements

Actual controls captured from the live app UI.

Together

Find a nearby device for Together. Long press forgets the last device.

Menu Toggle

Open or close the drawing tools menu.

Reset

Return to the full paper view. Long press cycles zoom stops.

Alert style

Cycle alert banners. Long press resets all tool settings.

Dark mode

Switch between light and dark mode.

Pencil

Limit drawing input to Apple Pencil or allow finger drawing again.

Undo

Undo the latest action. Long press redoes the latest undone action.

Clear

Clear the visible doodle. Long press clears saved sessions or requests a shared clear.

Export

Share a PNG or video. Long press saves a PNG or shares the .dtag recording.

Rainbow

Turn Rainbow drawing on or off. Long press edits Rainbow palette and style.

Colours

Open the colour palette for swatches, wheel, brightness, and recents. Long press opens Pencil setup.

Pencil

Cycle the current drawing size. Long press opens Thickness setup.

Eraser

Turn geometry erasing on or off. Long press edits Eraser size and strength.

Wind

Turn Wind shaping on or off. Long press edits radius and Wind mode.

Snap

Turn directional Snap drawing on or off.

Grid

Show or hide the square grid. Long press edits grid spacing and style.

Hex grid

Show or hide the hex grid. Long press edits hex cell size and style.

Recording

Start or stop a solo .dtag recording.
