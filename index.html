Doodle Tag

Draw together over Wi-Fi.

A shared sketchbook for two people in the same room.
Open Doodle Tag on two devices, connect, and draw together on one giant canvas!

No accounts. No invites. No cloud. Just doodles.

Playful tools

Plain and Outline pencils, Rainbow in Normal, Pop and Lanes, a true Eraser, Wind, guide grids, pan and zoom, PNG export, recording and MP4

iOS + Android together

Doodle Tag for iOS can connect with Doodle Tag for Android over the same Wi-Fi.

Private by design

Doodle Tag works locally over Wi-Fi. Your drawings stay between your devices and are never sent to external servers.
